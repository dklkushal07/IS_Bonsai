Founding Story

Shrubs was founded in 2023 by a group of bonsai enthusiasts who were passionate about sharing their love of bonsai with others. They saw a need for a platform that would make it easy for people to learn about bonsai and buy high-quality bonsai trees. At Shrubs, we are committed to promoting the bonsai culture and making it accessible to everyone. We offer a wide range of bonsai trees, all carefully selected for their quality and value. So if you're looking to buy a bonsai tree, connect with other bonsai enthusiasts, Shrubs is the place to be. Join us today and be part of a growing community of bonsai lovers from around the world!

Our Values

Creating a community of bonsai enthusiasts

Helping customers find the right bonsai tree to fit their needs

Providing information about the impact of technology in bonsai culture.

Inspiring people to create their own bonsai by showcasing our beautiful bonsai collection
